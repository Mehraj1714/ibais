MISSION

  • IBAIS University is a Private and non-profit educational institution. Its major commitments are to provide educational programs of international standard at undergraduate and graduate levels at the most reasonable cost to students.
  • The university programs are particularly responsive to the needs of individual citizens, public groups and agencies in Bangladesh.
  • The university aims to develop and pursue these programs so that the students who desire to develop and exploit their scholastic potentialities have the opportunity to do so. Respect for the intellectual potential and dignity of students as individual human beings, without prejudice to their racial, ethnic or cultural background, shall be paramount in all the academic and other activities of the University.
