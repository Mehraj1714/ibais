SCHOLARSHIP/STIPEND SCHEME
Stipend Scheme: (Only for Undergraduate Day Programs)
  1. Students having an average GPA of 4.75 and above but less than 5.00 in SSC and HSC examinations will obtain 40% stipend on total tuition fees.
  2. Those having an average GPA of 4.50 and above but less than 4.75 in SSC and HSC examinations will obtain 25% stipend on total tuition fees.
  3. Those having an average GPA of 4.0 and above but less than 4.5 in SSC and HSC examinations will obtain 15% stipend on total tuition fees.
  4. Those having an average GPA of 3.5 and above but less than 4.0 in SSC and HSC Examinations will obtain 10% stipend on total tuition fees.
  5. Those having an average GPA of 3.0 and above but less than 3.5 in SSC and HSC Examinations will obtain 5% stipend on total tuition fees.
  6. Those having an average GPA of 3.0 and above but less than 3.5 in SSC and HSC Examinations will obtain 5% stipend on total tuition fees.
  7. Students completing GCE ‘O’ and ‘A’ levels will obtain 40%, 25%, 15%, 10% & 5% stipend on total tuition fees for GPA of 4.75, 4.50, 4.0, 3.5 & 3.0 respectively [A=5.00, B=4.00, C=3.00, D=2 and E=1.00].
Tuition Fees Waiver: (For All Programs)
  1. Those having an average GPA of 3.0 and above but less than 3.5 in SSC and HSC Examinations will obtain 5% stipend on total tuition fees.
  2. Siblings studying at the same time at IBAIS University will receive 20% rebate on total tuition fees of one student
  3. The children of Freedom Fighters and Teachers will receive 10% rebate on total tuition fees.
  4. Female students will enjoy 10% rebate on total tuition fees.
  5. Siblings of any former student of IBAIS University will get 10% rebate on total tuition fees.
  6. IBAIS University Graduates will get Admission Fee waiver.
Full-free Studentship:
  1. Students having GPA 5.00 in SSC and HSC examinations will be offered full-free studentship. And students having GPA 5.00 in ‘O’ and ‘A’ levels will also be offered full-free studentship. But they have to pay the following fees:
    1. admission fee
    2. internship/ thesis/project fee
    3. examination fee in case of retaking the examination of any course
    4. 70% tuition fees of the respective course(s) in case of retaking course(s).
