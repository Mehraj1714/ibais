CUMULATIVE GRADE POINT AVERAGE

Two kinds of grade point average are used at IBAIS University. The CGPA is calculated by dividing the total earned grade points by the number of credit hours attempted in a given trimester. The earned grade point, is computed by multiplying the value of the grade point equivalent to the corresponding letter grade by the number of credit hours for the course (e.g. a student earning a B (3.0) grade in a 3 credit hours course earns 9.0 quality points).
