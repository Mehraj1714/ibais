MEMBERSHIP

Association of Collegiate Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP), Kansas, USA European Universities Continuing Education Network (EUCEN) (The Largest University Networks in Europe ) American Chamber of Commerce in Bangladesh (AmCham) The Oxford Association of Management (OXIM)

Benefits of Membership
ACBSP

  • The pursuit of membership reinforces a commitment to continuous improvement, innovation, and scholarship.
  • Faculty members get access to the online library of ACBSP.
  • Faculties are provided opportunities to keep current in quality procedures through seminars, trainings.
  • The pursuit of ACBSP membership enhances the ability to serve students by assuring a focus on quality performance.
  • Students can refer to membership status on résumés and in employment interviews.
  • Membership provides a vehicle to network with other institutions, gaining from insight and experiences of others.
  • Membership provides a voice for business education at the national and international levels.
  • Membership provides the availability of the Teaching Excellence Award.
  • Membership provides professional development and leadership opportunities for teachers and administrators.

    • ACBSP

  • Contribute to the development of an effective university credit transfer system that would be acceptable within the network.
  • Promote interaction in order to identify mobilize resources within the network.
  • Seek to harmonise levels of quality for the members and to maintain standards for effective monitoring.

    • AEI-NOOSR

  • AEI-NOOSR is an organization of Australian Government who evaluates the equivalence of foreign University degrees. Only those students who have completed their Bachelor and Master degree from the NOOSR’s recognized universities can directly admitted into the Masters and PhD programs respectively at Australian universities. Otherwise they have to study extra 1 (one) or 2 (two) semesters before starting their Masters and PhD programs.
  • For this recognition, IBAIS University students can directly take admission into any university of Australia. Moreover, they will get priority in migration and have career opportunities in various reputed organizations of Australia.

    • AmCham

  • To present the views of the AmCham members to the Government of Bangladesh.
  • To work through the Asia Pacific Council of American Chamber of Commerce (APCAC) in order to present the views of US business community in Bangladesh to the United States Government.
  • Sponsoring seminars on vital issues of finance, trade and commerce.
  • Organizing US Trade Show in Bangladesh on annual basis with the co-operation of the United States Embassy.

    • OXIM

  • The Oxford Association of Management is incorporated in England & Wales; registered with the United Kingdom register of Learning Providers.
  • OXIM recognizes the graduate and postgraduate degrees awarded by IBAIS University for certified memberships in UK.
